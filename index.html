About Me

I'm a student at Purdue University, pursuing Computer Science with an interest in web development and design. I love the intersection between art and computer science, and want to continue discovering new ways to implement my creativity into my code, where it be web designing, web development, creating graphics, or just learning how to code the front-end. Although I prefer the front-end aspect, I am also interested in learning the back-end. I am eager to learn more about the computing world while showing the world that computer science is more than just programming.

Besides coding, I enjoy designing, playing around on Adobe Illustrator, watching movies, and going on walks. I also love watching broadway shows, traveling, reading psychological thriller books, and coloring. Check out my design portfolio to see what I've created!
